Red Flag:The sender's email address is external to RMH. Always pay close attention to external emails to verify they are legitimate.

Red Flag:The email is creating a sense of urgency to click the link, which is typical of phishing emails. Are you actually expecting a delivery from FedEx?

Red Flag:The email is telling the recipient to click a link. Always examine links by hovering over the link to show the destination in an attempt to spot fraudulent links.

Red Flag:Did the FedEx Authentication Portal look legitimate? Fraudulent sites, quickly created for malicious purposes, sometimes look very basic in comparison to their real counterparts.
